HOW TO USE THIS APP

Welcome to our Smoke Detector Outreach Locator!

Use this map to determine where to target smoke detector outreach:

  • Choose which levels of fire risk you'd like to include, from 1 (low) to 10 (high)
  • Check which type of location you'd like to use for your outreach
  • Choose a buffer size to see how many people your outreach can potentially impact
  • Hit "Update Map"
Reset the map to clear the data.

Other information:

  • Hover over a grid cell to get data about that particular area
  • Population data is interpolated from US Census information

This application uses predicted fire risk data from an algorithm built by University of Pennsylvania masters students. To read more about how fire risk has been defined and what motivated this project, please hit "Back to Musa" to read our report.
